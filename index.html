OUR VISION

TO DEVELOP COMPETENT AND CONFFIDENT LEARNERS WITH MORAL & SOCAIL VALUES, WHO COULD THINK CRITICALLY FOR THE LARGER INTEREST OF THE SOCAIETY.



OUR MISSION

COMMITTED TO THE HIGHEST STANDARDS OF ACADEMIC EXCELLENCE, WE DEDICATE OURSELVES TO PROVIDE THE BEST POSSIBLE EDUCATION FOR OUR STUDENTS BY DEVELOPING THE KNOWLEDGE, ATTITUDES, AND SKILLS ESSENTIAL FOR LIFE-LONG LEARNING.UTILIZING THE AVAILABLE RESOURCES WITH THE ACTIVE AND EFFECTIVE INVOLVERMENT OF STUDENTS, TEACHERS AND PARENTS THROUGH STRATEGIC PLANS AND APPROPRIATE ACTIVITIES IN A VERY CONDUECIVE ENVIRONMENT USING THE APPROPRIATE AND SUITABLE APPROACHES / METHODS WITH THE ASSISTANCES OF PROPER MONITORING, FOLLOW-UP AND FEEDBACK MECHANISM TO STEER THE EDUCATIONAL PROCESS TOWARD THE RIGHT DIRECTION.
